Sip tea on a sunlit balcony or wander through a bustling souk close by — but enjoy your time at Magnolias because we want you to embrace the magic and joy of our homes, just like we have. 

After all, the magic of finding a home away from home was exactly why MagnoliasVHR was founded in December 2022. The family who founded it promised and continues to promise the warmth of Southern hospitality and the vibrant soul of Dubai within its halls, all built on the belief that every journey, every trip, and every small visit is meant to be transformative — and this trip can, too. 

Our dedication to this promise has represented us in the Traveller’s Review Awards for 2023 and 2024, and we proudly put those forth as a testament to our incredible guests and community for supporting our dream of making Magnolias Vacation Homes Rental—a place where unforgettable experiences begin.
